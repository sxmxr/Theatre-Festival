About University

Ahmedabad University is a private, non-profit research university that offers students a liberal education focused on interdisciplinary learning and research thinking. We mould independent thinkers and compassionate leaders who go on to engage innovatively with the complex challenges of our societies.

University Website

About Theatre Club

Theatre Club is one of the most prominent student clubs working in the University. We strive to promote different ideas of theatre by conducting various events interconnected with fun and enlightenment. We focus on building young audiences into creative, curious, and compassionate adults. We aim at providing our audiences with engaging, insightful, and entertaining events, along with building their social skills, confidence, and enriching skills.

About The Festival

With the unpredictable changes that we all experienced in 2020 caused by the pandemic, It is now time to endorse these virtual times in 2021 by merely adjusting to it. With the pandemic reducing the amount of physical theatre and technology bringing the whole world together, we,Theatre Club of Ahmedabad University are organizing a two-day-long Nationwide virtual Theatre Festival. This festival will kindle the “hope of a new dawn” in our audience, through exciting events lined up like

  • Insightful Talks
  • Round-Table Talk
  • National level monologue competition
  • Masterclass on Improvisation
  • A new normal play
  • Location Zoom
  • Date & Time 10am - 7pm, 6-7th March
  • Artists 200+ Performers
  • Audience 2000+ Spectators

10.00 - 11.30 AM Inaugral Event

The rise of the dawn

With the end of an era, comes the rise of a new dawn

With the beginning of the theatre festival highlighting “Moving towards a new dawn”, an enriching conversation about the rise of theatre might be the only thing you need to hop onto hope. We promise you a journey of theatre from the decades and what is all set to change in the upcoming years.

Location: Zoom
12.00 - 3.30 PM Competition

National level Monoact Competition

Get ready to win some exciting hampers.

An enriching competition across the nation for the student community filled with monologues, mono acts, mimes, and soliloquies. The event will unfold from submission of the mono acts to a live virtual mono-act competition. Not just that, exciting prizes and a certificate of appreciation await the top 3 mono act winners.

Location: Zoom
7.00 - 8.30 PM Performance

Kahaani Night

Dive into the realm of stories

We aren’t sure about a dive into the ocean, but we are all set for a virtual dive into an evening filled with storytellers and enthusiastic performers from Ahmedabad university.

Location: Zoom
10.00 - 11.30 AM Workshop

Improve your Improv

A Master Class on Improvization

“When something goes wrong in your life, just yell ‘Plot Twist’ and move on.” This workshop is all made for you if you just said “yes” to this. Improvisation is not only meant for the world of actors but will help you develop adaptability for everyday situations. Your successful completion of the workshop will guarantee your certificate of participation.

Location: Zoom
12.00 - 1.30 PM Round-table talk

The curtains reopened

Stage redefined across the nation

A roundtable conference with Theatre artists from different parts of the nation taking us through the journey of regional theatre and the essence of it in the current times. The conversation will also revolve around how “stage” continued to redefine itself for decades and predict the route that it will take in the new normal times.

Location: Zoom
5.00 - 6.30 PM Play Showcase

New normal one-act play

A play showcase by ahmedabad University students

A virtual showcase of socially relevant as well as emotionally engaging play, written, directed, and performed by artists from Ahmedabad University.
